Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pick up my food?

Check your "My Orders" tab. Once the status changes to SIAP, you can go to the respective stall and present your Order ID to collect your items.

My payment failed, but my balance was deducted!

In the rare event of a network glitch, our payment gateway reconciles all failed transactions within 24 hours. Your funds will be automatically refunded.
